What is Pointube?
Meet your new shopping partner.

Pointube is your ‘Personal Point Wallet’ which show your outstanding point and mile data, transmit from participating Reward Program Operators via secured access.

Once registered with Pointube, you will know how many points and miles you have for spending. From there, you can comfortably browse the redemption offers featured in Pointube. We present to you a variety of offers your points and miles can buy.

Don’t let your points and miles go un-used. The more you redeem, the more you save!
